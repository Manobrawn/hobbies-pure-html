What are Hobbies and why is it important?

Hobbies are activities that people engage in during their free time for enjoyment and relaxation. They provide a break from daily routines, allowing individuals to explore interests, develop new skills, and reduce stress. Hobbies are important because they improve mental well-being, enhance creativity, and offer opportunities for personal growth and social connection.
